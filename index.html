About Me

Hi! I'm Travis. I am a graphic designer and developer with experience in print, digital, and web. For eight years after college, I worked in communications and marketing at a couple non-profits before joining the Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, IN. Now I am combining all of these acquired skills to create products that the customer (and myself) can be proud of.

Giving back and using my skills for good are important to me. That is why I volunteer as the communications person at my church and freelance design work for non-profits in my community as a side project. If you are looking for someone who is fun, a fast learner, and hard worker, then I'm your guy!
